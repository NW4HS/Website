About Sinematiko

Celebrating Filipino Stories Through Digital Innovation

Our Mission

Sinematiko is dedicated to promoting and preserving Filipino culture through the power of cinema. We believe that every Filipino story deserves to be told, shared, and celebrated. Our platform provides a dedicated space for Filipino filmmakers, content creators, and audiences to connect through the universal language of storytelling.

What We Offer

As a Filipino-focused streaming platform, Sinematiko offers an extensive library of movies, television series, and documentaries that showcase the rich tapestry of Philippine culture. From classic films that defined generations to contemporary stories that reflect modern Filipino life, our collection spans across genres and eras.

Our Vision

We envision a world where Filipino content is accessible to audiences everywhere, where our stories reach beyond borders, and where the Filipino voice in cinema is recognized and celebrated globally. Through cutting-edge streaming technology and a commitment to quality, we're making this vision a reality.

Technology & Innovation

Built on a robust cloud-based infrastructure utilizing AWS, Kubernetes, and modern web technologies, Sinematiko ensures seamless streaming experiences across all devices. Our platform leverages PostgreSQL and MongoDB for efficient data management, Redis for caching, and CDN edge servers for fast, reliable content delivery whether you're watching on your smartphone, computer, or smart TV.

Supporting Filipino Creators

Beyond being a streaming platform, Sinematiko is a partner to Filipino content creators. We provide a sustainable model for filmmakers to monetize their work while reaching wider audiences. By choosing Sinematiko, you're directly supporting the Filipino creative industry and helping ensure that more Filipino stories continue to be told.
